CHILLI PANEER



Chilli Paneer is one of the most popular recipes in Indo-Chinese cuisine. It is simply flour or batter coated fried paneer cubes tossed in a spicy, salty, tangy and sweet sauce made with green bell peppers, garlic, ginger, green chillies, soy sauce and more seasonings. Crunchy paneer pieces in a sweet, spicy sauce - YUM!! Order it now and enjoy with your friends and family!
